Dream Tee

Social impact project where foster kids' artwork is transformed into high-end T-Shirts, empowering them through creativity while raising awareness and funding for their future.

Website Design
Copywriting

Overview

DreamTee turns foster kids' dreams into wearable art, empowering them through creativity. This project not only gives them a platform for self-expression but also funds their future by raising awareness through high-end T-shirts.

The Problem

Many foster children lack the resources and platform to express their creativity. DreamTee bridges this gap by offering them a chance to share their dreams with the world.

Solution

By providing art kits and turning their designs into T-shirts, DreamTee empowers foster children and creates a lasting impact on their lives, while also raising funds for their future education.
