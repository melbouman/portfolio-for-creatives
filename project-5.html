Dream Tee

Social impact project where foster kids' artwork is transformed into high-end T-Shirts, empowering them through creativity while raising awareness and funding for their future.

Website Design
Copywriting

Overview

Everyone deserves the chance to chase their dreams, especially when they're young, but some don't have that opportunity, and that's when it matters most. At DreamTee, we turn foster kids' dreams into wearable art. Every design is created by a child expressing their emotions and aspirations through art, helping them build confidence and inner peace. By wearing our shirts, you’re not just making a fashion statement—you’re supporting their journey, funding their future, and sharing their stories with the world. Together, we’re fostering creativity and creating brighter tomorrows.

The Problem

Many kids, especially in underserved communities, don’t have the resources to be able to pursue their artistic passions.

Why don’t foster kids have the same advantage as their peers to pursue art?

I gathered information through interviews and discussions with people directly involved in foster care. This is what I discovered...
Lack of Awareness - Access Barriers - Financial Challenges

Solution

Children in foster care often face significant emotional and social challenges. Many lack consistent outlets to express their feelings or develop confidence. Studies show that creativity is a powerful tool for promoting mental health and healing—it helps children process their emotions and share their unique perspectives. DreamTee is a nonprofit designed to empower foster children by turning their creativity into something tangible. Every step of the journey is designed to make them feel valued and supported. Foster kids get special art kits to make their own cool designs. Then, their artwork is uploaded and printed onto real shirts that people can wear.
