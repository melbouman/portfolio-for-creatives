Dream Tee

Social impact project where foster kids' artwork is transformed into high-end T-Shirts, empowering them through creativity while raising awareness and funding for their future.

My Role
Website Design Copywriting

At DreamTee, we turn foster kids' dreams into wearable art. Every design is created by a child expressing their emotions and aspirations through art, helping them build confidence and inner peace. By wearing our shirts, you’re not just making a fashion statement—you’re supporting their journey, funding their future, and sharing their stories with the world. Together, we’re fostering creativity and creating brighter tomorrows.
