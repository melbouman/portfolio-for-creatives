Small Changes

Tackling Climate Anxiety Through Workshops Website

Website Design
Copywriting

Overview

We want to bridge the gap between the experts and their specific audience. We aren’t looking to convert climate deniers but use the knowledge and insights from the experts in a powerful way that combats the barriers they face.

Role

Collaborated in a team of two to develop brand identity; independently led the website design to attract users and elevate the brand’s presence online.

Timeline

January 2025 – March 2025

The Problem

How might we get individuals to attach themselves personally to climate change? Experts are struggling to move their audience from education to action. What are the major barriers they face?

Research

We conducted interviews with university professors and students to understand what motivates people to act. Many young adults are overwhelmed by data and misinformation, resulting in climate anxiety. Through behavioral psychology, we explored ways to reduce this anxiety and turn education into engagement.

Solution

We designed an interactive roadmap exhibit and website to guide young adults toward their first steps in climate action, transforming abstract concern into practical involvement.
