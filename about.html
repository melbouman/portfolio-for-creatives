      Never Not Designing

      Ever since I was a kid, I’ve always been a designer at heart. Whether it was drawing on the corners of my notebooks or building little creations with whatever I could find, I was constantly imagining and shaping the world around me. Design felt like a natural way to express my ideas, and I’d spend hours experimenting with colors, shapes, and textures. Over time, this passion only grew, becoming more than just a hobby—it turned into a way for me to communicate, solve problems, and make things better. Designing has always been part of who I am.
