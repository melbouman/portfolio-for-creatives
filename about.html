A bit about me

I’m a UX/UI designer passionate about crafting intuitive and visually compelling digital experiences. With a focus on seamless interactions and thoughtful design, I help brands connect with their audiences in meaningful, human-centered ways.

I grew up on Vancouver Island, where my creative roots began in fine arts. From painting on blank canvases to designing digital ones, I’ve always loved telling stories through visuals. Discovering UX/UI felt like the missing piece — where creativity meets purpose. I’m driven by research, obsessed with the details, and always curious about what the user needs, thinks, and feels.
