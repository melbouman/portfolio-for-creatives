Paws & Places

Location finder app to discover dog-friendly places and businesses in different parts of town.

My Role
Branding, app design, copywriting

Not everyone loves dogs, nor are they welcome everywhere. Vancouver's dog lovers want to include their four-legged friends in almost all their outings. They want their dogs to have as much as they do. Paws and Places lets them discover dog-friendly places and businesses in different parts of town. Knowing where and when dogs are welcome lets the stewards plan their dogs days so everyone can get along with minimum conflict and maximum fun.visible as you scroll down the page.

The Problem

Dog owners in Vancouver often struggle to find easily accessible information about pet-friendly places to bring their furry friends. The lack of a centralized resource for this information can leave them feeling frustrated and even guilty, as they strive to include their pets in as many activities and outings as possible.
