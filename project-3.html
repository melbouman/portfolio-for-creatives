Paws & Places

Role: Branding App design Copywriting.

Not everyone loves dogs, nor are they welcome everywhere. Vancouver's dog lovers want to include their four-legged friends in almost all their outings. They want their dogs to have as much as they do. Paws and Places lets them discover dog-friendly places and businesses in different parts of town. Knowing where and when dogs are welcome lets the stewards plan their dogs days so everyone can get along with minimum conflict and maximum fun. visible as you scroll down the page.
