Melly

Streetwear E-commerce Website for Skateboarders

E-commerce Website Design
Copywriting

Overview

Streetwear is all about self-expression no matter what age you are. Melly is a streetwear brand that lets skaters as well as fashionistas show the world that they are on top of the latest trends. Messy handwriting and a graffiti-inspired wordmark signal that street style rules Melly’s fashion sense.

Role

UX/UI Designer — I led user research, crafted wireframes and prototypes, and created a visual identity that reflects both empowerment and luxury.

Timeline

January 2025 – March 2025

The Problem

The challenge lies in creating an authentic digital space that reflects the raw energy of streetwear culture while maintaining a user-friendly shopping experience.

Research

I explored the aesthetics of various streetwear brands and analyzed user behavior to understand how to effectively merge edgy visuals with seamless navigation.

Competitors

Analyzed leading streetwear platforms to identify trends, strengths, and pain points to ensure Melly stands out with unique branding and an intuitive user experience.

Solution

A bold, visually dynamic platform with easy navigation and personalized user experiences that captures the essence of street culture.
