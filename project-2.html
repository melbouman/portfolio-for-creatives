Overview

Streetwear is all about self expression no matter what age you are. Melly is a streetwear brand that lets skaters as well as fashionistas show the world that they are on top of the latest trends. Messy hand writing and a graffiti inspired word-mark signal that street style rules Melly’s fashion sense.

The Problem

The challenge lies in creating a brand that captures the spirit of Vancouver’s skate and urban culture while offering unique, high-quality designs that empower wearers to express themselves confidently and authentically..
